Notebookify

Top Spotify Stats and Graph Generator

Your Notebooks
Provides you with personalized notebooks of your top tracks, artists, and genres.
You can sort the notebooks based on short term (previous month), medium term (previous six months), and long term (all time) data.
The number of rows displayed in your notebook (10, 50, or 98) can also be customized.
An track/artist's popularity score relative to other creators is rated out of 100.
Playlist Graphs
Provides you with a list of your playlists. From this list of playlists, select one to obtain graphs of various data inside that specific playlist.
Graphs include a map of the playlist's artists/albums/tracks, release dates, track tempos, artist frequency, track keys, and more!
A track/artist's popularity score relative to other creators is rated out of 100. The valence of a track is how much of a "feel-good" vibe the song has to it.
